Hello, my name is Jordan. I am a indegnious canadian from the Piikani Nation. I was born and raised in the windy city of Lethbirdge, Alberta. Currently I have been studying in a Multi Media Program, at the Lethbridge College. I plan on finishing post secondary because I want to pursue a career blending artistic abilities and my newly learned technical technology skills. I worked as a intern at the Lethbirdge University radio station, CKXU 88.3 FM. My time there, peaked my interest in sound production and help inspire me to take a Multi Media Program at the College.

FutureBirdziview is my musical alias I choose, because I consider myself to have a birds eye view persepective and my the future aspect is homage to my favourite anime character Future Trunks from the Dragon Ball Z anime series. Music is a big part of my life, I am not spending my free time working on my instrumental music, then I am most likely listening too music to find inspiration. I have a vast music collection, I enjoy listening to 70's and 80's Jazz Fusion, Disco, Funk, Rock, Soul, and Rnb music, I also am a hip hop head. Hip hop artist known as RZA, Lordfubu, Clams Casino and MF Doom are my influences when I make my beats. As I create synth melodies and mixed them with aggressive hip hop drums. I have been making beats for roughly 4 years and only as of this past year would consider my production quality. My friend who goes my Quidiculus is a rapper from Alaska and we have been collorating, I always enjoy the chance to be a music producer and hope to create visual art for my music production in the future.
